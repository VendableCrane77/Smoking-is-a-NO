Why do we purposely breathe in what we try to avoid from a different source?

Harmful Chemicals in Cigaretts and Vapes/ Games & Quizzes

When you smoke, you breathe in Carbon Monoxide,
which is the same Carbon Monoxide everyone tries to avoid in exhaust and campfires.
So why does every smoker think it is alright with the same gas from something even more toxic?

The answer lies in Nicotine, a substance found in Tobacco that can cause addiction.
However, it is not a mental addiction, but a chemical addiction.
Though, Nicotine is not the only chemical that can cause problems.

For example, since Carbon Monoxide can bond as easily as Oxygen with Hemoglobin,
a protein that helps carry the oxygen to all parts of the body,
Carbon Monoxide can take the place of Oxygen,
needing the heart to pump oxygenated blood to the rest of the body faster, so the body will not deprive of Oxygen.
This can cause Tachycardia and others, which can lead to death.

Due to its cause for so many problems, many governments around the globe
 have decided to ban the use of cigarettes and vapes.

The particles released in the smoke are pm2.5, 30% of the size of a red blood cell,
can cause a variation of lung disease like COPD and lung cancer.
Therfore, since 1970 and 2018 respectively, Singapore has
banned the use of cigarettes and vapes.

However, Singaporean designers have created a designated space for smoking.

If you are caught smoking at undesaignated areas, you can be sentenced to a fine of up to SGD$1000.
If caught vaping, you can be sentenced to a fine of up to SGD$10 000 or jail of up to 6 months or both.

The most important information you should know before smoking is that
Tobacco kills more than 8 million people a year,
which is about 50% of ita users.

Therefore, before you light a cigarette bud or vape up,
remember how many people were killed because of the same action you are about to start.

When people smoke, it can be like starting an engine that cannot stop,
or like Pamela Worth who managed to quit smoking.

Therefore, if you want to keep fit, remember the words of Maxi Lim,
starring as Aloysius Jin in the Ah Boys to Men Movies:"My secret is no cigarette."
